The art of organic movement
NatureMind speaks about the freedom to be and to visit between characters and to experince their world.
The style is characterized by characters from a multi-dimensional world, with pastoral inspiration, who tell a story using organic body language, and outfit...

״I believe that the dancer (and the viewer) has an inner bond with the world of the character, and her role is to conveying him a journey and telling him about her part in him.
So that process of creating the character touches the soul of the dancer and creates a spiritual process with the virtues of healing.”

Julia offers courses, workshops and private lessons in NatureMind style.
