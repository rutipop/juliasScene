Welcome to
"Julia's Scene" Dance | Art | Fashion
The site gives you a glimpse into my scene - a scene that present multidisciplinary art which includes dance, painting and fashion. All of these were born by a special worldview that sees art as a holistic field, that has different representations, and therefor reflected in a whole range of themes. The diversity in "doing" all above is based on the perception of which we live in a world where we must create, breathe and move.

Who am I?
Born in 87, married to Eldad and mother to Lenny.
A Dancer. An artist. A creator.

Dancer
For the past 17 years I have been performing on stages, in Israel and abroad. I started my studies at Nadine Boomer's Academy of Dance, and in 2000 I became a lead dancer in the dance troupe "Nadine Animato". The group performed in Israel and abroad at many large festivals, including in Germany, the USA and the Netherlands.

Artist
In recent years I have developed the art of movement called "Nature Mind". The method introduces the new language of movement through characters and short stories, telling their story in silent movement. The method emphasizes organic movement, flexibility and improvisation, and it brings to the stage the value of the imagination as a central value to the work. The method can be learned in a variety of workshops or individually frontally and zoom.

Creator
Good dance and proper movement need flexibility, release and comfort. Mostly because of this, I designed a collection of dance and sportswear outfits, for teenagers and elderly women. The collection is made in Israel, in the highest qualities, and it allows everyone who wears it feeling complete harmony with her body.

At the site, you will also find my collection of paintings, created by me, which features spectacular and colorful figures. If you find them suitable - this is the place to order and renew.

Remember that" imagination is the beginning of creation" (George Bernard Shaw)

All yours, Julia Galya Cohen.
